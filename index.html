✨ AI auto-fill

Paste an Anthropic API key and the app can read bottle labels and invoices for you. The key is stored only on this device and is sent directly to Anthropic — nowhere else.

These AI settings are shared with everyone in your cellar, so you only set them up once.
Get an API key from the Anthropic Console →
Tip: in the Console you can set a monthly spend limit on the key for peace of mind. Reading a label or invoice typically costs a fraction of a penny.

👥 Share your cellar

To share with your partner: they install the app and sign in once with their own Google account (this creates their account). They send you their User ID; you add it below. Then you give them your Cellar ID and they tap Join. After that you both see and edit the same cellar.

Make sure they've added your User ID first. Joining switches you to their shared cellar (your current view is replaced).

📄 Import from invoice

Take a photo of a wine invoice or pick a PDF. The app reads the wines and prices so you can add them all at once. Check the details before adding.
